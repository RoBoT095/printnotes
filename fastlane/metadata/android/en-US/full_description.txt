<p><i>Print(Notes)</i> is a cross-platform markdown notes app inspired by Google Keep and Obsidian.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Supports Markdown... obviously</li><li>Supports LaTeX used for math notation: <a href='https://quickref.me/latex' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>cheatsheet</a></li><li>Stores notes locally on device</li><li>Possible to create folders to better organize notes</li><li>Can view images and pdf files found in selected directory and in external folders</li><li>Has many colors to choose from by default</li><li>Changeable layout and sort order</li><li>Toolbar for easy markdown editing (borrowed from <a href='https://github.com/zahniar88/simple_markdown_editor' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>simple_markdown_editor</a>)</li><li>Create your own custom color schemes to better personalize it to your liking</li></ul>
